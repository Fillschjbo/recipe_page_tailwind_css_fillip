Chicken Tikka Masala

Created by CurryKing

2025-15-04

Tikka Masala is a popular Indian-inspired dish made with marinated pieces of meat (usually chicken) cooked in a creamy, spiced tomato-based sauce. It's known for its rich flavor, vibrant orange color, and smooth texture, often served with rice or naan.

Step by Step

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    • Mix chicken, yogurt, and spices
    • Marinate 30 min to overnight
  2. Cook the Chicken:
    • Heat oil, cook chicken until browned
    • Set aside
  3. Make the Sauce:
    • Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger
    • Add tomato purée and crushed tomatoes
    • Simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Combine:
    • Add cream and chicken back in
    • Simmer 5–10 minutes
    • Season to taste
  5. Serve:
    • Top with coriander
    • Serve with rice or naan
